Prehistoric Treasures

Are you ready to play a Match 3 game? Prehistoric Treasures gives you 6 rounds to collect the gems for each ticket.

Try to get 3 identical gems in a round to win the associated prize. If you get an Instant Win gem, your win the associated prize immediately.

HOW TO BET

Ticket Cost

Displays the current ticket cost.

Press the left arrow (–) to decrease the ticket cost.

Press the right arrow (+) to increase the ticket cost.

Press BUY to deduct the ticket cost from the balance and play the game.

[top]

HOW TO PLAY

Reveal All

Press REVEAL ALL to automatically play the game to completion. Game play continues until all selections have been made.

[top]

About The Game

The object of the game is to get three identical gems or get an Instant Win gem to win the associated prize.

Game Screen

After a ticket is purchased, the game displays a 6-round ticket. Each round contains 5 stones with associated prize. (unrevealed at the beginning)

Winning a Prize

Click stones or press REVEAL ALL button to reveal the gems. If three identical gems in this round are revealed, the associated prize will be awarded.

If an Instant Win gem is revealed, the associated prize in this round will be awarded.

Game Completion

When all the stones and prize values are revealed, the game ends. A closing message appears, indicating the game’s outcome.
